The Three Pillars of Our Comprehensive SEO Audit

Technical SEO Audit

We check for crawlability/indexability issues (robots.txt, sitemaps), review server configuration, and test your website's performance, including Page Speed and Core Web Vitals, which are major ranking factors.

On-Page SEO Audit

A meticulous review of your content quality, keyword usage, meta tags (titles/descriptions), heading structure (H1-H3), and internal linking. We ensure every page is optimized to match user intent.

Off-Page SEO & Backlinks

An analysis of your backlink profile to assess domain authority and trust. We identify weak or spam-heavy links that could be dragging down your authority and suggest link-building opportunities.

Competitor Analysis

A comparative review to see how your site matches up against top-ranking competitors in terms of content, link profile, and positioning to identify gaps and opportunities you can close quickly.

Mobile & Usability Review

We check to ensure your site is mobile-friendly, with readable text, properly spaced elements, and a responsive design, aligning with Google's mobile-first indexing standards.

Actionable Report & Prioritization

You receive a comprehensive report that segments issues into errors, warnings, and notices, so you know exactly which critical items to fix first to see immediate results.
