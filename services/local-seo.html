Our Local SEO Strategy: The Road to the Map Pack

Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization

We'll optimize your GBP with a clear description, consistent Name, Address, Phone number (NAP), accurate hours, and high-quality photos to improve your relevance and prominence in local search.

NAP Citation Building

We ensure your business Name, Address, and Phone number are consistent across all major online directories and citations. Small oversights in NAP consistency can cost you in rankings and audience perception.

Local Landing Pages

We create dedicated, location-specific pages for each service area, including unique content, location-based keywords, embedded Google Maps, and local testimonials/reviews.

Local Content Strategy

We develop locally relevant content like city-specific guides, blog posts about local events, and FAQ pages that address the needs of local searchers and build authority in your community.

Location-Based Keyword Optimization

We target keywords that combine your service with where you do it, integrating them into your URLs, title tags, and headers to reinforce topical and location relevance.

Local Link Building

We earn high-authority local backlinks by partnering with local businesses, sponsoring local events, or engaging in community outreach to strengthen your domain authority.
